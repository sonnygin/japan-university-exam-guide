# Cold Email Template Reference

## General Constraints

- Tone: Highly polite, professional academic language. No exaggeration or flattery.
- Both versions convey the same core content but read naturally in their respective languages. Do NOT produce literal translations.

---

## English Version

### Constraints

- Word limit: 400 words max
- Language: English only

### Structure

#### Subject Line

If professor specifies a required format, follow it exactly. Otherwise use:

```
Inquiry regarding Admission: [Proposed Topic Keywords] - [Applicant Name]
```

#### Paragraph 1: Self-Introduction (2-3 sentences)

Briefly introduce name, current university, major/program, and expected graduation. Keep factual and concise.

**Example:**
```
Dear Professor [Name],

My name is [Name], and I am currently a [year] student majoring in [Major]
at [University]. I am writing to express my sincere interest in pursuing
graduate studies under your supervision at [Target University].
```

#### Paragraph 2: The Hook (3-5 sentences)

This is the core value proposition. Must include:
1. Reference to a specific recent paper or project by the professor (by title or topic)
2. A 1-2 sentence proposed research direction that bridges the professor's work with the applicant's background
3. Brief mention of how the applicant's skills/experience can support this direction

**Example:**
```
I was particularly inspired by your recent work on [Paper Title / Topic],
which addresses [brief description]. Building on this, I am interested in
exploring [proposed direction], an area where my experience in [relevant
skill/project] could contribute meaningfully. During my studies, I have
[specific achievement or experience relevant to the direction].
```

#### Paragraph 3: Call to Action (2-3 sentences)

Politely ask about:
1. Whether the professor is accepting new students
2. Permission to send a detailed CV or research proposal

**Example:**
```
I would be grateful to learn whether you anticipate having openings for
graduate students in the coming academic year. If so, I would be happy to
share my detailed CV and a preliminary research proposal for your
consideration. Thank you very much for your time, and I look forward to
hearing from you.

Sincerely,
[Name]
```

## Chinese Version (中文版)

### Constraints

- 字数限制：500 字以内
- 语言：正式学术中文，避免过度客套或网络用语

### Structure

#### 邮件标题

若教授页面指定了标题格式，严格遵守。否则使用：

```
修士入学咨询：[研究方向关键词] - [申请者姓名]
```

#### 第一段：自我介绍（2-3 句）

简要介绍姓名、在读院校、专业、预计毕业时间。

**示例：**
```
[教授姓名] 教授，您好：

我是 [姓名]，目前就读于 [大学名称] [专业名称]，预计于 [毕业时间] 毕业。
我对您的研究方向非常感兴趣，希望能有机会在 [目标大学] 攻读修士课程，
在您的指导下开展研究。
```

#### 第二段：核心价值（3-5 句）

必须包含：
1. 提及教授近期一篇具体论文或研究项目（写出标题或主题）
2. 用 1-2 句话提出一个将教授研究与申请者背景结合的研究方向
3. 简要说明申请者的相关技能或经历如何支撑该方向

**示例：**
```
我拜读了您近期关于 [论文标题/研究主题] 的工作，该研究在 [简述贡献] 方面
令我深受启发。结合我在 [相关技能/项目] 方面的经验，我希望能在 [具体研究方向]
方面进行探索。在本科期间，我曾 [具体的相关成果或经历]。
```

#### 第三段：礼貌收尾（2-3 句）

询问：
1. 教授是否计划招收新的修士学生
2. 是否方便发送详细简历或研究计划

**示例：**
```
冒昧打扰，想请问您在下一学年是否有招收修士学生的计划？如果方便的话，
我希望能将详细的个人简历和初步研究计划发送给您参考。
感谢您百忙之中阅读此邮件，期待您的回复。

此致
敬礼
[姓名]
```

---

## Red-Line Checks Before Drafting (Both Versions)

Before writing either version, verify:
1. Does the professor's page specify a required email subject format?
2. Does the professor require applicants to read a specific guide first?
3. Does the professor require a specific form or attachment?
4. Does the professor explicitly state they are NOT accepting students?

If any special requirement is found, it takes **absolute priority** over the default template.
